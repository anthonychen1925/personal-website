Cerebrospinal Fluid Single-Cell RNA Sequencing ML Disease Classifier

Developed a disease classifier using 1000+ single-cell RNA-seq cerebrospinal fluid datasets (GEO). Processed data with AnnData/Scanpy, used UMAP for visualization and PCA for dimensionality reduction, and trained a scikit-learn Random Forest model achieving ~70% accuracy.
